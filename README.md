# customer-churn-analysis
Dataset
The dataset includes the following columns:
Customer ID – Unique identifier for each customer
Subscription Date – Date the customer subscribed
Status – Active or inactive
Revenue – Sales revenue per transaction
Churn Indicator – Whether the customer has churned
Analysis Performed
Data Preparation
Imported Sales and Products tables into Excel
Cleaned data: checked dates, removed duplicates
Added a calculated column for Revenue = Quantity × UnitPrice × (1 – Discount)
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Revenue
Number of Orders
Average Discount
Total Quantity Sold
Visualizations
Monthly Revenue Trend – Line chart
Revenue by Category – Column chart
Top 5 Products by Revenue – Bar chart
Interactivity
Added a Slicer for filtering by Category or City
Added a Timeline for filtering by OrderDate
Target Comparison
Joined with Targets table
Created a chart comparing Actual Revenue vs. Target by Category
Dashboard Layout
KPIs positioned at the top
Charts arranged in the middle
Slicers placed on the side
Applied consistent colors and formatting for clarity
Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Formulas: IF, COUNTIF, SUMIF
 What will be demonstrated in the Excel file:
Clean and structured sales data ready for analysis
Calculated KPIs for business insights
Interactive visualizations with slicers and timelines
Comparison of actual performance versus targets
Professional dashboard layout for clear presentation
